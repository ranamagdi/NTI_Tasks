Yes. Whenever a link is used on your website that results in a commission, you must disclose this information to site visitors, according to FTC rules. We suggest adding something like: "Disclosure: I am an affiliate of Fortune.org where your purchases support local bookstores. I will earn a commission if you click through and make a purchase." to your website or post.

Affiliates do not have to handle inventory, fulfillment, shipping, or customer service. All book sales are fulfilled by Ingram. Ingram's extensive inventory and regional warehouse system ensures Fortune.org that books can reach customers in as soon as 2-3 days.

All affiliates can withdraw their revenue whenever they like via Stripe using an easy web interface, provided they have a balance of $20 or more.Fortune participating in the revenue share receive payments every six months.
